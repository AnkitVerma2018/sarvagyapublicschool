From the Principal's Desk

“Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectations”.

Sarvagya Public School is an amalgamation of competent teachers, state-of-the-art infrastructure and an experienced and efficient administration. The school provides a supportive environment for its students to ensure a perfect balance of academics, sports, artistic and social opportunities.

Childhood, youth and learning should be a joy and we strive for that. We foster a positive spirit and believe in partnership between students, parents, teachers and support staff to create a milieu that sustains excellence.
